Property

As one of the leading property solicitors in Northern Ireland operating out of offices in Belfast, Newtownards, Holywood, Ballymena, Comber and Kircubbin, our team of specialist property (conveyancing) solicitors can guide you through the legal process of buying, selling or remortgaging your property. We are a member of the Law Society Home Charter Scheme which means that we meet the high standards the Law Society sets to ensure that we give clients a professional and quality conveyancing service. Our priorities are to make you feel comfortable with us and confident that your move will go to plan at a reasonable price.


We will be pro-active on your behalf to ensure the moving process runs as smoothly as possible, keeping you informed at all stages along the way so that the house moving experience is as stress free as possible. Our property team are very experienced and have seen or dealt with almost every problem or situation which can arise when moving home. A high percentage of our work comes from repeat business or recommendations which is testament to our professional, friendly and effective service.
