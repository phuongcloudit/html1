About Us

In 1902 Alex Stewart, grandfather of Stewarts’ current Senior Director Charles E Stewart, and great-grandfather of Charlie PW Stewart (Director) commenced practising in Newtownards.

The practice was known as Alexander Stewart and provided legal services to the people of Newtownards and the surrounding rural hinterland for many years. In 1940 Alex Stewart was succeeded by his son William C. Stewart who continued to practice under the name of Alex Stewart and Son until 1974, when the practice was in turn passed to his son, Charles E. Stewart.

In 1999, following a restructuring and rebranding, the practice commenced trading as a partnership under the name of Stewarts Solicitors. In 2008 Norman Scott who had practised for many years as Senior Partner in the firm known as F.J. Orr & Company joined Stewarts. On the 2nd September 2013, Stewarts Solicitors became incorporated pursuant to the Solicitors’ (Incorporated Practice) Regulations 2007 (‘the Regulations’) of the Law Society of Northern Ireland.

In 2015 the firm expanded its reach in the North Down and Ards area by opening a full-time office in Holywood and, with John Campbell of Campbell Bates & Company Solicitors joining, the firm again has a presence in the town of Comber. “Over the last 114 years our firm has developed from a small country practice into thriving province wide law firm with an experienced team of specialised solicitors and support staff.” “We seek to adapt to meet all challenges and improve our service to clients. The new generation together with the experienced staff members many with over 20 years’ experience will strive to accomplish these aims.” “As life itself changes, so must the legal profession to meet the fresh challenges ahead and working with our clients we can be confident of achieving their goals.” Charles E. Stewart

Work With Us

Stewarts is a continually evolving law firm keen to attract Solicitors and support staff of all levels who have an ambition to succeed and a drive to develop within the firm.

We are keen to receive approaches from established legal professionals wishing to ply their trade and support their existing clients within an efficient, proactive and modern firm.

We are also keen to receive approaches from sole practitioners or practitioners nearing retirement who wish to have a secure footing and support for their loyal clients. This is evidenced by Norman Scott, formerly Senior Partner in FJ Orr & Company, Belfast joining the Firm in 2008 and John Campbell, formerly trading on his own account as Campbell, Bates & Company, Comber joining the Firm in 2015.

Stewarts is registered with the Law Society of England & Wales and can therefore offer training contracts to persons who have completed the appropriate LPC course.

Our internal training system ensures that it is possible to progress within the firm for employees who show interest, enthusiasm, willingness to learn and application. We keep a confidential record of all Curriculum Vitae received and continually review these when a position becomes available.
